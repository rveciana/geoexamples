Benefits of working with tiles

  • Easy
  • Lots of examples, standards, data and libraries
  • Ability of representing huge amount of data
  • Data pre-process

Problems of working with tiles

  • Server load or space
  • Little flexibility in projections
  • Difficult interactivity
  • The integration with other parts of the web is more limited

  • Reduces the size up to a 80%
  • Allows polygon simplification without problems at the borders
